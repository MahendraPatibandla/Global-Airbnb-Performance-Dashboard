# 🌍 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, aimed at analyzing Airbnb listings across major cities worldwide.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand:

* Market distribution
* Pricing behavior
* Customer satisfaction
* Growth trends

It transforms raw data into **actionable business insights** through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The primary goal of this project is to answer key business questions such as:

### 🔍 Big Picture / Overview

* How large is Airbnb’s presence across major global cities?
* How has Airbnb grown over time and where did it peak?
* Which cities contribute the most to Airbnb’s overall business?
* Are listings evenly distributed or concentrated?

---

### 🏙️ Market Share by City

* Which cities dominate Airbnb listings globally?
* Are a few cities driving most of the supply?
* What percentage of total listings do top cities control?
* Is Airbnb supply fragmented or concentrated?

👉 Insight: Helps identify **growth focus areas and strategic markets**

---

### 💰 Property Type & Pricing Analysis

* Which property types are most commonly listed?
* Are hotel rooms more expensive than Airbnb stays?
* Why do travelers prefer Airbnb over hotels in certain cities?
* Does pricing influence higher adoption in cities like Paris?

👉 Insight: Explains **pricing strategy and customer preference**

---

### ⭐ Ratings & Customer Satisfaction

* Which cities provide the best guest experience?
* Which cities are underperforming?
* Are low ratings driven by:

  * Cleanliness
  * Value for money
  * Communication?
* Does higher supply always mean better quality?

👉 Insight: Identifies **quality gaps and improvement areas**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive filters (City, Property Type, etc.)

* Trend analysis over time
